Production and Trade

  • One pound of the spice is priced at US$1000 and takes the collection of about 75,000 flowers (Masi et al., 2016).
  • The cultivation and collection process is manual and labor intensive (Díez-Méndez and Rivas, 2017).
  • Major producers that contribute to world’s harvest of saffron are Iran, Greece, Morocco, India (J&K) and Spain.
  • In addition, it is also being cultivated in Turkey, France, Switzerland, Israel, Pakistan, China, Egypt, United Arab Emirates, Japan, Afghanistan, Iraq and Australia (Tasmania) (Yasmin and Nehvi, 2013; Díez-Méndez and Rivas, 2017).
